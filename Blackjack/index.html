Dealer and Player
A game of Blackjack involves a player, a dealer, and a 52 card deck. The dealer gets 2 face up cards and a face down card. The player gets 2 face up cards. The goal is for the card values to add up to 21.

Numbered cards are worth their value, any non-numbered card is worth 10 points apart from the ace.

Decision
Now, the player gets to choose whether he wants to hit, or stand. If hit, the player gains another face up card and may do this as many times as they want. However, if they go above 21 then they go "bust" and lose the game. If they choose to stand, the dealer reveals his face up card and the player who's closest to 21 wins. However, if the dealer goes above 21 then the player wins.
